How it works

Save in seconds.
Track for real.

Stackd encourages disciplined saving while making progress visible — on your desk and in your pocket.

01

Set your goal in the app

Create a vacation fund, emergency fund, car fund, house fund, or anything custom. Choose your savings mode and how visible you want your progress to be.

02

Deposit on the device

Tap your card for the amount you chose in the app, or slide bills into Stackd Up. Stackd counts, verifies, and deposits value into your linked account.

03

Watch the bar fill up

The front LED progress bar grows with every deposit — $100 of $1,000 is 10%, $500 is 50%. Hit 100% and the entire bar illuminates, plus an app notification.

Savings modes

Save on your terms.

Pick the level of commitment that fits your goal — from wide open to fully locked.

Locked until goal

Money cannot be withdrawn until the savings goal is completed. Saving $2,000? You can't withdraw at $1,500 — unlocks only when you hit $2,000.

Locked until date

Christmas, tuition, vacation — pick a date and your funds unlock automatically when it arrives. Ideal for time-bound savings targets.

Standard savings

Deposit anytime, withdraw anytime. No restrictions — just a beautiful way to save with visible progress on your desk.

Early withdrawal fee — Remove money before your goal or lock date? A small penalty applies. Built to encourage commitment and reduce impulse withdrawals.
