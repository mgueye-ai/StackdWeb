Savings modes

Save on your terms.

Locked until goal

Funds stay put until you hit your target. Saving $2,000? You can't withdraw at $1,500 — unlocks only at $2,000.

Locked until date

Christmas, tuition, vacation — pick a date and your money unlocks automatically when it arrives.

Standard savings

Deposit anytime, withdraw anytime. No restrictions — just a beautiful way to save with visible progress.

Early withdrawal fee — Break a locked commitment before your goal or date? A small penalty applies. Built to reduce impulse withdrawals and keep you committed.
