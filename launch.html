FAQs

How is Stackd different from a regular savings app?

Savings apps are easy to ignore — Stackd creates physical presence. The LED bar on your desk makes progress visible every day, and lock modes help you stay committed.

What's included with a Stackd purchase?

Your purchase includes the physical device and full access to the Stackd app — no subscriptions, no extra fees. Buy once, use forever.

What's the difference between Stackd and Stackd Up?

Stackd ($49) handles card tap deposits with an LED progress bar. Stackd Up ($99) adds a built-in cash slot that counts, validates, and deposits bills automatically.

How does Stackd connect to my bank account?

Stackd uses banking infrastructure powered by Unit. When you set up the app, you link a bank account securely — deposits from your device flow into that linked account.

What are savings modes?

Choose standard (flexible), locked until a date, or locked until you hit your goal. Early withdrawal from locked modes may include a small penalty to encourage commitment.

Is checkout secure?

Yes. Payments are processed through Stripe. We never store your card details on our servers.
